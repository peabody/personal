Funny Stuff with Free/Open Source Software

Often I find the real appeal of Free and Open Source software is its humanity, as demonstrated in the humerous witicism hidden throughout it's nooks crannies.

Emacs

  • Try evaluating this in your scratch buffer with Emacs: (use-global-map (make-sparse-keymap)). Instantly unusable Emacs :-D. Reference: http://www.dina.kvl.dk/~abraham/religion/poor-mans-vi-mode.html
  • If you've never read the Antinews for Emacs, you should, it's a kick. It's a sarcastic tongue-in-cheek listing of previous Emacs features as "upgrades" should you wish to use a previous version of Emacs. M-x info g Emacs <ENT> m Antinews.
